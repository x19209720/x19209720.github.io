Coffee and a kitty— Find a new best friend

Hello, we're Purr Playground, Dublin's first Cat Cafe. Located in the heart of Temple Bar, we have the best coffee and coolest cats for you to hang out with. Who knows? You may end up meeting your new best friend and adopting one of our lovable shelter kitties!

Our Cats

All our cats are ethically sourced from Dublin shelters in order to provide them with food, shelter and pets from you cat lovers in order to give them a second shot at life! More information on our cute buddies is available at the top of the page.

Donate

We do all we can for Dublin's Animal shelters by donating 10% of every cafe item sold going straight back into funding the welfare of these animals. We also welcome donations of food, cat beds, toys and money to keep our mission up and running.
